Questions

Good To Know

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Every plan is billed monthly or annually with no lock-in — manage or cancel from My Account at any time.
Do workouts repeat?
No two days feel the same — the daily set rotates between Sprint, Distance and Mixed Endurance themes, scaled to your chosen level.
I've never swum competitively — is this for me?
Absolutely. The Beginner track focuses on fundamentals, water confidence and stroke mechanics before any race-pace work.
Do I need special gear to start?
No — goggles and a suit are enough to begin. Fins, a kickboard, pull buoy, paddles and a snorkel are recommended as you progress; every set tells you exactly what to bring.
How does AED billing work?
All plans are priced and billed directly in UAE Dirhams (AED) through our secure MemberSpace checkout — no currency conversion surprises.
