Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Every plan is billed monthly or annually with no lock-in — just email us anytime to manage or cancel.
Does the workout generator repeat?
No — it computes fresh sets from whatever discipline, distance, equipment and goal you choose, so the same inputs plus a different level or goal always produce a different set.
I've never swum competitively — is this for me?
Absolutely. The Beginner level scales every generated set down to fundamentals, water confidence and stroke mechanics before any race-pace work.
Do I need special gear to start?
No — goggles and a suit are enough. Leave every equipment box unchecked and the generator builds a plain-swim set; check off fins, a board, a buoy or paddles as you acquire them.
How does AED billing work?
All plans are priced and billed directly in UAE Dirhams (AED) through our secure checkout — no currency conversion surprises.
